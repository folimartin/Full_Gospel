About

WHO WE ARE

“Let there be real harmony among you so there won’t be any split in the church.

I plead with you to be of one mind, united in thought and purpose.” 1 Corinthians 1:10 (LB).

Man was made to last forever. He was made to have:

  1. A purpose to live for.
  2. People to live with.
  3. Principles to live by.
  4. A profession to live out.
  5. Power to live on.
Our Mission

"To bring people to Jesus for membership in His family.
Build them to maturity and train them for ministry in the church and send them to fulfil their mission in the world in order to magnify God’s name by leading people to be devoted to Christ,
building relationship with one another and influencing their community for Christ by those who have lost a mate by death, etc..."

Our Vision

“To reach and equip our generation to discover their purpose in life, using contemporary, creative, and caring ways that will translate into a one-million-member church of transformed and devoted Christians with countless branches globally...”
